The Disappearing Billboard

The Concept
A dissolvable digital billboard at high-footfall hotspots - Piccadilly Circus, Times Square, Connaught Place. Every 30 seconds, the card details disappear and a fresh card generates. The Revolut logo and tagline stay fixed on screen. The card never does.
Why It Works
Every card dissolve is a potential shareable moment on social - time-lapses write themselves
Conventional media hook: a billboard that never shows the same card twice
First digital OOH that demonstrates its product in real time
Campaign Line - stays on screen always
