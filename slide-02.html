02 / Audience

Meet the Potential ICPs for Phantom

Four existing Revolut customer personas. All active online shoppers. None using single-use cards yet.
Here's why they need a Phantom in their life.

Zoe
The Impulse Explorer
Age 24-29
Role Marketing / Creator
Income 35K-55K
"I found this lamp on an Instagram ad at midnight. The site looked fine? I just entered my card and hoped for the best."
Shopping Behaviour
Instagram / TikTok ads DTC brands 4-6x / week
Pain Points
  • 30+ sites have her card saved. Forgotten most of them.
  • Mystery charges from sites she cannot even recall.
  • Card compromised once. Updating everything was painful.
Why Phantom
Shop impulsively, without the anxiety tax.
"Shop fearlessly. Your card forgets, even if you don't."
Max
The Borderless Buyer
Age 26-33
Role Freelance Photographer
Income 30K-50K
"I paid for a street food tour in Marrakech, a scooter rental in Bali, and a hostel in Tbilisi. All on different local sites. All in the last two weeks. Every one of them has my real card number."
Shopping Behaviour
Street-side vendors Local booking sites Foreign transport apps 10-15x / month
Pain Points
  • Pays on local sites with zero security standards.
  • Charged 3x by a tuk-tuk app in Bangkok. No support page.
  • Cannot track which vendor in which country has his details.
Why Phantom
Every new city, every street vendor gets a card that dies when he moves on.
"Travel everywhere. Leave nothing behind."
Kai
The High-Stakes Player
Age 22-28
Role Developer / Designer
Income 40K-65K
"I bid on a vintage watch on an auction site I had never heard of. Won it for 180 euros. Great price. But now some random platform in Estonia has my full card details."
Shopping Behaviour
Auction sites Resale platforms P2P marketplaces 3-8x / month
Pain Points
  • Paying strangers on unverified platforms with real card details.
  • No way to know how securely a marketplace stores data.
  • Double-charged by a resale site with zero customer support.
Why Phantom
Win the bid, get the item, leave no trace to exploit.
"Play anywhere. Trust no one. Pay everything."
Tom
The Subscription Skeptic
Age 25-32
Role UX Designer / Startup Ops
Income 45K-70K
"Wanted to try a fitness app for 7 days free. Three months later they had been charging me 14.99 a month. Did not even open it after day 2."
Shopping Behaviour
Free trials SaaS tools Streaming apps 2-3x / month
Pain Points
  • Auto-renewals from trials he forgot to cancel.
  • Cancellation flows designed to be deliberately confusing.
  • Monthly statements full of small mystery charges.
Why Phantom
The trial ends when HE decides. Not when he remembers to cancel.
"Free trials that are actually free."
