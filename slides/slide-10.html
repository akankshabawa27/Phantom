The Phantom Magic Show

The Concept
Partner with a world-famous illusionist (David Blaine or Dynamo) and host a Phantom Magic Show at high-footfall hotspots: Covent Garden London, Las Ramblas Barcelona, Times Square NYC. Every trick ends the same way - the card disappears. Punchline every time: "Powered by Phantom by Revolut. The card that is built to disappear."
How It Works
Targeted Revolut users receive the invite as a card-shaped note printed on the same dissolving paper - the product experience starts before they arrive.
Target users of Revolut who already use the virtual debit card.
To spread the word further, plant creators in the crowd to capture the gasps - the hero Reel goes live 48 hours post-show.
✦ The Kicker
It crumbles in their hand.
At the end of each trick, the magician hands the audience a real Phantom card printed on water-soluble paper. Body heat starts the timer. In 30 seconds it's gone - nothing left to hold.
