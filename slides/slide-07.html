07 / Creative Platform

The Creative Pitch

The Big Idea
Phantom is the first payment product built on the principle of deliberate impermanence. It does not protect your card details. It eliminates them. In a world designed to remember everything, Phantom is built to forget.
Campaign Tagline
Made to Disappear.
Pay. Then Poof.
Parent and child. "Made to Disappear" is the campaign platform. "Pay. Then Poof." is the playful execution line for social, Stories, and in-app moments.
Connection to Zoe
Zoe doesn't want to be "protected." She wants to be free. Phantom removes the consequences of being adventurous. "Made to Disappear" is not a warning. It's a permission slip.
The Rationale
01
Insight
We are security-obsessed in every part of our digital lives except the one moment that actually costs us money.
02
Tension
Card details outlive the transaction. They sit in forgotten databases, waiting to be reused, breached, or charged again.
03
Product Truth
Phantom generates fresh card details for every transaction. The moment you pay, the old details stop existing.
04
Creative Leap
Don't market this as "security" — passive and forgettable. Position it as a deliberate act of digital impermanence. Not a feature. A statement.
